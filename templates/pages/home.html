✥ Recent Projects ✥

✥ RFID/NFC Exploration ✥

It can read, emulate, and sometimes clone RFID or NFC tags, which is useful for understanding access control systems and security research (always on devices you have permission to test).

✥ Infrared (IR) Control ✥

Flipper Zero can capture and replay IR signals from remote controls (e.g., TVs, air conditioners) to help understand how these devices communicate.


✥ Penetration Testing ✥

The device provides a portable platform to test vulnerabilities in systems you own or have explicit permission to test, helping to improve overall security.
